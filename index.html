After five years of operation, the FutureGrid project has come to an end on September 30, 2014.
Funded by the NSF, FutureGrid operated a geographically distributed set of heterogeneous computing systems, and enabled research and education projects in a variety of topics in computer science and across various application domains. Throughout its funded period, FutureGrid hosted XXX projects and YYY users, and the project enabled research leading to ZZZ publications and WWW presentations. This static page replaces the FutureGrid portal, summarizes our accomplishments and provides information to users of FutureGrid and those interested in using similar infrastructures.
We would like to thank users for their input and insights and for making FutureGrid a very successful project.
